The Way — how it all connects

One circle, read three ways: the zodiac (the heavens), the Cosmic Clock (the initiations), the Round Table (your selves). The quest moves as a figure-eight:

① The heavens turn the wheel — the planets ride the signs, and each seat they light presses its test on the world. (That is Today's Quest.)
② The challenge arises in your life — not as punishment but as the mirror: the outer event shows you the knight-self seated at that station.
③ You speak the invocation — the nine-verse decree is the outgoing current, your call rising from below to Above.
④ The masters answer within — the return current: dreams, promptings, the quiet knowing. The Dream Gate reads their letters.
⑤ Seeing, you surrender the seat — the self is not slain but let die; the seat turns gold; the Cup at the centre fills.
⑥ At twelve, the centre opens — the Siege Perilous, the seat of the Christ, and the Grail is recovered — for it was the consciousness you became.

Call and answer, Above and below, out and back — the figure-eight. The invocations are your voice to the masters; the dreams are their voice in you.

The Vigil — bring an issue to the masters

Before a knight rode out, he kept the night-watch. Describe the issue, the resistance, the situation you keep meeting — in your own words. It is read in the light of the teachings, the masters answer, and you receive a decree to give daily until the answer comes — often as a dream. When it does, bring the dream to the Dream Gate and it is joined to this working.

Your workings

Each working is a figure-eight in motion: the daily decree is the outgoing current; the dream, the prompting, the shift is the return current. Keep the decree daily and watch the nights.

The Dream Gate

The quest sends its signposts through the night. Write the dream as you remember it — even a fragment. The gate reads its symbols, names the station it points to, and gives you a decree to carry.

The Dream Diary

Every dream kept as a sealed letter of the masters' reply. Dreams come in sequence — the diary shows the threads that recur.

The Oracle — talk with Merlin

Merlin — the guide the corpus names as the master who was Roger Bacon and Francis Bacon, Saint Germain — will talk with you. Ask about what's arising in your life, the seats, the teachings, the way. He remembers the thread and answers as the counsel of the quest.
