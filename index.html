The Way — how it all connects

One circle, read three ways: the zodiac (the heavens), the Cosmic Clock (the initiations), the Round Table (your selves). The quest moves as a figure-eight:

① The heavens turn the wheel — the planets ride the signs, and each seat they light presses its test on the world. (That is Today's Quest.)
② The challenge arises in your life — not as punishment but as the mirror: the outer event shows you the knight-self seated at that station.
③ You speak the invocation — the nine-verse decree is the outgoing current, your call rising from below to Above.
④ The masters answer within — the return current: dreams, promptings, the quiet knowing. The Dream Gate reads their letters.
⑤ Seeing, you surrender the seat — the self is not slain but let die; the seat turns gold; the Cup at the centre fills.
⑥ At twelve, the centre opens — the Siege Perilous, the seat of the Christ, and the Grail is recovered — for it was the consciousness you became.

Call and answer, Above and below, out and back — the figure-eight. The invocations are your voice to the masters; the dreams are their voice in you.
